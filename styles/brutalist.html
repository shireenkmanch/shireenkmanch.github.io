Machine learning researcher working on epistemic uncertainty in modern AI systems: models that quantify what they do not know and act accordingly.

Lead research at Oxford Dynamics on reasoning in foundation models and decision-making under uncertainty. Targets mission-critical environments where models cannot afford to be confidently wrong.
