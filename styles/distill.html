Shireen Kudukkil Manchingal

Building AI that knows when it does not know.

Researcher, Oxford Dynamics · Oxford, UK

I am a machine learning researcher working on epistemic uncertainty in modern AI systems: models that quantify what they do not know and act accordingly. I lead research at Oxford Dynamics on reasoning in foundation models and decision-making under uncertainty.

The work targets mission-critical environments where models cannot afford to be confidently wrong, and where knowing when to defer, abstain, or escalate is as important as producing an answer.
