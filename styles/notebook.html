Shireen Kudukkil Manchingal

Building AI that knows when it does not know.

researcher, oxford dynamics // oxford, uk

I am a machine learning researcher working on epistemic uncertainty in modern AI systems: models that quantify what they do not know and act accordingly.

I lead research at Oxford Dynamics on reasoning in foundation models and decision-making under uncertainty. The work targets mission-critical environments where models cannot afford to be confidently wrong.
